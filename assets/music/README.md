# Music Assets

Put background music files here.

Accepted formats:

- .mp3
- .wav
- .m4a
- .aac
- .ogg

Rules:

- Use royalty-free or owned music only.
- Keep background music subtle under narration.
- Recommended mood: chaotic, playful, fake news, fake documentary, tech comedy.
- Default volume should stay low around 0.025 to 0.045.
